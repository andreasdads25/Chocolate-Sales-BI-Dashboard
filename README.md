**Chocolate Sales BI Dashboard** <br><br>


End-to-End Business Intelligence Project | Python | Streamlit | SQL | ETL | PDF Reporting <br><br>


**Overview** <br><br>
The Chocolate Sales BI Dashboard is an end‑to‑end Business Intelligence project that integrates: <br><br>

- A full ETL pipeline in Python <br><br>

- Data cleaning & transformation <br><br>

- An interactive Streamlit dashboard <br><br>

- KPI calculations <br><br>

- Automated PDF reporting <br><br>

- Actionable business insights for the CEO & Sales Manager <br><br>


The project is designed with consulting‑grade standards to demonstrate real business value and serve as a complete portfolio piece for BI/Data Analyst roles. <br><br>

**Business Scenario** <br><br>
The company Chocolate Factory has been experiencing unstable sales performance, and the CEO requested a unified BI dashboard to answer key business questions:

- Which products generate the highest profitability? <br><br>

- Which regions perform best? <br><br>

- How do monthly sales evolve over time? <br><br>

- Which customers contribute the most revenue? <br><br>

- What trends should the Sales Manager monitor to make informed decisions? <br><br>

This dashboard helps: <br><br>

- the CEO gain a high‑level overview, <br><br>

- the Sales Manager identify opportunities, <br><br>

- the Operations Manager forecast demand. <br><br>

**Business Insights Summary** <br><br>

- 65% of total revenue comes from just three products. <br><br>

- The North region delivers the highest profitability. <br><br>

- Sales show a clear upward trend during Q3–Q4. <br><br>

- 20% of customers generate 70% of the revenue (Pareto effect). <br><br>

- There is a strong opportunity for cross‑selling in low‑engagement regions. <br><br>
